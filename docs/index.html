What is REX?

Robotics Experience Program (REX) is a unique learning platform hosted by IEEE RAS SBC RIT with the objective to strengthen student's skills and knowledge in robotics and automation through a month-long virtual specialization program. The event opens up the opportunity to interact with reputed personalities to build a strong base in ML, AR, IoT and 3D printing through specialization courses and interactive sessions. The event offers 30+ workshops, 10+ lecture series and a virtual IV for participants around the world to share, exchange, and establish knowledge in robotics and automation.
