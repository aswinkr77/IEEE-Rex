What is REX?

Robotics Experience Program (REX) is a unique learning platform hosted by IEEE RAS SBC RIT with the objective to strengthen student's skills and knowledge in robotics and automation through a month-long virtual specialization program. The event opens up the opportunity to interact with reputed personalities to build a strong base in ML, AR, IoT and 3D printing through specialization courses and interactive sessions. The event offers 30+ workshops, 10+ lecture series and a virtual IV for participants around the world to share, exchange, and establish knowledge in robotics and automation.

Our speakers

KRISHNANAND KAIPA

Krishnanand Kaipa is a robotics enthusiast, versatile mentor, and a distinguished engineer who is currently an associate professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace engineering at the Old Dominion University. He pursued a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology in the year 2007. He has 22+ years experience in the field of robotics and his broad research is focused on developing robots that learn from, and work side-by-side with humans. His research areas of interest involve imitation learning, swarm intelligence, evolutionary active learning, advanced manufacturing, motion planning etc. He is the recipient of the Best PhD Thesis and Best Masters Thesis from the Department of Aerospace, IISc. He has also issued 27+ publications on various field of robotics.
